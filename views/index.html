"Making Sweet Memories"



Party planning has always been a passion of mine. I will always remember my mother setting up decorations and a nice table spread for every occasion. My mother and I planned out my entire wedding and we enjoyed every minute of it. She always spoke about us one day having our own event planning business once she retired. Sadly, shortly after my father passed away, which was around the time of her retirement, my mother joined him. My best kept encouraging me to fulfill that dream in her honor, and in April 2017, close to her birthday, Honey's Sweet Memories LLC was created.. "Honey" was my mothers nickname given by all the grandchildren and it stuck and today, I have been able to create and make sweet memories for many!
